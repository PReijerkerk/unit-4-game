Crystal Collector

Instructions

A random number is given at the start of each game.

There are four crystals below. Clicking a crystal will add a specific amount of points towards your point total.

You win the game by matching your point total to the random number assigned at game start. If your score goes above the random number you will lose the game.

Each new game will assign a different amount of points to each crystal.
